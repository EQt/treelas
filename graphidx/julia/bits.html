    Bit Manipulations

    GraphIdx.Bits.hyperfloor — Function.
    hyperfloor(x)

    Heighest power of 2 that is smaller than x.

    Example

    julia> bitstring(UInt8(123))
"01111011"

julia> bitstring(UInt8(hyperfloor(123)))
"01000000"
