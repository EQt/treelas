    GraphIdx.Grid.iter_edges — Method.
    iter_edges(proc, n1, n2, dirs)

    Call proc(u::Int, v::Int, len::Float64) for every grid edge u -- v having length. Hereby u and v are the index of the corresponding grid-nodes.
